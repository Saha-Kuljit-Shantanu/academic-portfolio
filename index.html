    Saha Kuljit Shantanu is a full-time lecturer in the department of Computer Science and Engineering since July 2025, at International University of Business Agriculture and Technology (IUBAT). He earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). His area of interest lies in Human Computer Interaction (HCI), primarily in the intersection of AI and pedagogy.
